# Privacy Policy – BarterStore

**Effective Date:** 14 December 2025

BarterStore (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the BarterStore mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- Email address for authentication
- User-generated content (posts, images, descriptions)
- City/location (manual or auto-detected if permission is granted)
- Device and usage data for analytics and crash reporting

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide marketplace features
- To show nearby listings
- To improve app performance
- To display advertisements

## 3. Ads & Third-Party Services
BarterStore uses:
- Google AdMob
- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Firestore & Storage
- Firebase Analytics

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell or rent user data. Data is shared only to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Data Security
We use industry-standard security practices, but no system is completely secure.

## 6. User Rights
Users can edit or delete posts, control app permissions, or request account deletion.

## 7. Children’s Privacy
BarterStore is intended for users **13 years and above**.

## 8. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated from time to time.

## 9. Contact Us
Email: support@barterstore.in

## Account and Data Deletion

BarterStore respects user privacy and provides a clear process for account and data deletion.

### How to request account deletion
Users can request deletion of their BarterStore account and associated data by emailing us at:

**Email:** support@barterstore.in  
**Subject:** Account Deletion Request – BarterStore

### Data that will be deleted
Upon verification, the following data will be permanently deleted:
- User account details (email, phone number, profile)
- Posts and listings created by the user
- Chat messages and conversations
- Uploaded images and related content

### Data retention period
Data will be deleted within **7–30 days** of request, unless retention is required by law.

### Contact
For questions related to data deletion, 
contact: support@barterstore.in
